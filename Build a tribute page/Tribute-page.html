The Prophet Muhammad SAW

Here's a time line of The Prophet Muhammad SAW's life:

  • 570 - Born in Mecca, Hejaz, Arabia.
  • 595 - Married with Khadija, a wealthy businesswoman who had staked out a successful career as a merchant in the caravan trade industry.
  • 610 - Visited by Gabriel in the cave and receiving his first revelation from God.
  • 613 - Start preaching revelations publicly.
  • 622 - The event of Hijrah, where he and his followers migrated from Mecca to Medina (then known as Yathrib)
  • 629 - Gathered an army of 10.000 muslim converts and marched on the city of Mecca
  • 632 - A few months after returning from the Farewell Pilgrimage, he fell ill and died.
